rov_nmb_sm_controller module¶

class rov_nmb_sm_controller.ROV_NMB_SMController[source]¶

Bases: uuv_control_interfaces.dp_controller_base.DPControllerBase

Model-free sliding mode controller based on the work published in [1] and [2], or model-free high order sliding mode controller.

[1] Garcia-Valdovinos, Luis Govinda, et al. “Modelling, design and robust
control of a remotely operated underwater vehicle.” International Journal of Advanced Robotic Systems 11.1 (2014): 1.
[2] Salgado-Jimenez, Tomas, Luis G. Garcia-Valdovinos, and Guillermo
Delgado-Ramirez. “Control of ROVs using a Model-free 2nd-Order Sliding Mode Approach.” Sliding Mode Control (2011): 347-368.
