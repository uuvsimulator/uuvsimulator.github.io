Vehicles available
==================

.. _rexrov:

RexROV
------

This is the test vehicle available in the UUV simulator package. The RexROV is a fully thruster actuated remotely operated vehicle for which the dimensions
and hydrodynamic parameters are derived from the parameters for the SF 30k ROV available in :cite:`Berg_2012`.
